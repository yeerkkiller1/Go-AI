Board Representation

The board is represented as a one-dimensional array.

Neighbors of a point can be computed with offsets WE and NS, so that the four neighbors of point p are: 

               p + SG_NS
    p - SG_WE      p      p + SG_WE
               p - SG_NS

The board is surrounded by one line of border points (if size is SG_MAX_SIZE) in all directions; also diagonally out from the corners.

Coordinates

SgPointUtil::Pt, SgPointUtil::Row, and SgPointUtil::Col can be used to convert between points and coordinates. The coordinates start with 1 and the conversion is independent of the board size. Pt(1, 1), which corresponds to A1, is the lower left corner of the board.
